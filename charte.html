The ERODIA Guild

Presentation and Objectives

Hello everyone, The Erodia guild is a sociable guild, the objective is to browse the PvE / PvP content with the various members of it but also to be able to offer, to whoever wishes, the possibility of discovering the Tales Of Wind content in its entirety, at your own pace. We wish make every Guild Member feel at home and we put everything implemented to achieve and respect this aspect. So that each person can take full advantage of the Erodia Guild and what it offers, we want certain rules are observed by all.

The guild's vision

The administrators of the Erodia guild wish to constitute a community of self-respecting and learning members one another. Respect and tolerance are fundamental and everything is about discriminatory will be accompanied by a sanction according to the latter. We're here to have fun all together!

Functioning and knowing how to be

Chapter 1: Mutual aid

The entire staff team wishes above all that mutual aid be a primary objective common to each, there are players of different levels, we rely on the most experienced to be able to answer questions from beginner (es). Do not hesitate to ask questions even if they seem silly to you, it is by forging that you become a blacksmith !.

2nd Chapter: Respect

We are a sociable guild which aims to be in communication with the members of this one, but let's not forget that while some have known each other for quite some time and allow themselves to vanish on "sensitive" subjects, that some people are more sensitive than others on certain subjects, so we ask to be suitable with your fellow guild sisters. So are you trolling in a good mood and get to know each other before hiring a sensitive ground.

3rd Chapter: Name of staff members

We are making this staff organization chart available, please feel free to MP on Discord if you have the slightest question concerning the guild (technical questions, complaints, ideas ...)

4th Chapter: Main Commandments

1) You will be present: In order to maintain the dynamic atmosphere of the guild, we want to have members active and regularly connected, for all absences, you can obviously prevent any staff member without you justify, just by telling us your departure date and if possible your return date

2) The word you will avoid cutting: In order to be able to have a harmonious and pleasant discord, we wish that each member can express themselves clearly and audibly by everyone. Cutting off someone who is speaking will only lead to frustration and the inability to make themselves understood.

3) New member you will welcome: In order to facilitate the integration of a new member, we invite members already present to make an effort to welcome a new member of the guild, answering their questions, helping them and sharing vocally.

4) Your class you will learn: So that you can be comfortable in the game with your guild mates, we invite you to browse the guides (present on the site or other media) that will help you improve yourself in the game and thus master your class and its potential as well as possible

Chapter 5: Learning: the sinews of war

A guild that wants to advance is above all a guild that gives itself the means to progress, for that, no miracle, it is necessary to play, to learn and especially to understand. In order to facilitate the learning of the various contents that Tales Of Winds offers us we will provide videos that will guide you throughout your journey, the Card builds, Stuff sets but also PVP and PVE Skill Rotations, the goal being not to set aside players who want to progress significantl

6th Chapter: Guild Events

1) GVG, Dragon Temple :

The Dragon Quest launches every Tuesday at 7 p.m. Remember to leave a place for the kidnapped spouse for the bonus stats. The Temple of Light launches every Friday at 9 p.m. For GVGs, send your availability to the Captain of the guild 1 day in advance (Monday for Tuesday, Wednesday for Thursday) so that he can make the appropriate strategies for win against other guilds. The teams will be announced at 3 p.m. on D-day and the teams must be made at from 7:50 p.m. to get ahead.

2) Raccoons, Ball, CoCo, Ruin Boss

For the pups, make at least 2 teams of 3 in order to be as efficient as possible. At the Guild Ball, this is definitely the best time to use the music boxes in a group. There is a big XP buff over normal time If Coco is full, the Captain will send a message a day in advance to party the next day at 7 p.m. (except the Tuesday or there is the Quest of the Dragon) And finally the Bosses of the Ruins. They appear every Friday and Saturday at 2 p.m. and 10 p.m. To have the best bidding possible, the guild must at least be top 1 in DPS out of 5 bosses out of 16. So you have to be active the most possible during this event.

7th Chapter: Acknowledgments

Thank you all for carefully reading the Guild Charter, we understand that there are redundant and annoying points to read but in order to have a functional and pleasant guild we owe it to ourselves to make various rules! Take care of yourself and see you soon IN-GAME.
